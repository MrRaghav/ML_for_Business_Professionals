You can transform images from one domain,
such as photographs to another domain such as paintings.
This is called style transfer,
and you can use it to convert photographs to blueprints,
convert blueprints to photographs,
to correct colors in underwater photographs.
ML style transfer, isn't just limited to static images.
Here you can see the styles being applied
and adjusted to video in real time.
